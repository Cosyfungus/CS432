How to find your VIN

Your VIN (Vehicle Identification Number) is a 17 digit number and can be found in any of the following ways.

 

Insurance Card

You can find your VIN on the insurance identification card issued by your insurance company.

 

  

Vehicle Registration Card

You can find your VIN on the vehicle registration card issued by the department of Motor vehicles.

 

Top of the Dash

You can find your VIN on a metal plate on your dashboard near the windshield of the driver's side.

 

  

Driver Side Doorjamb

You can find your VIN on a lable on the driver-side doorjamb.
