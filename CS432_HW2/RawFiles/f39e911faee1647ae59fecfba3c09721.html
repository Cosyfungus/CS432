PERFORMANCE

SO POWERFUL, YOU’LL FEEL UNSTOPPABLE

There’s a certain power that comes when every choice you make is the right one. That’s the case with the Silverado Pickup Truck, where four durable engines — including the TurboMax — deliver the capability you rely on every day.

TurboMax™

Standard on WT, Custom, Custom Trail Boss, LT, RST and LT Trail Boss.

With 310 horsepower and 430 lb.-ft. of maximum torque, the TurboMax engine offers impressive power and no-compromise durability. Torque on demand is delivered by the unique dual volute turbocharger, which has priority oiling and cooling to help you take on the toughest jobs with ease.

  • 5.3L EcoTec3 V8

    Standard on LTZ and High Country; available on WT, Custom Trail Boss, LT, RST and LT Trail Boss.

    Boasting 355 horsepower with 383 lb.-ft. of torque, it’s ready to take on almost any task that comes its way.

    6.2L EcoTec3 V8

    Available on RST (4x4), LT Trail Boss, LTZ (4x4), ZR2 and High Country (4x4).

    With 420 horsepower and 460 lb.-ft. of torque, this engine makes short work of big jobs — on the jobsite or on the weekend.

    Duramax® 3.0L Turbo-Diesel

    Standard on ZR2. Available on Custom Trail Boss, LT, RST, LT Trail Boss, LTZ and High Country.

    As efficient as it is hardworking, the available Duramax 3.0L Turbo-Diesel engine offers 305 horsepower and 495 lb.-ft. of torque with 2WD towing of up to 13,300 lbs.†

TOWING AND TRAILERING

AS SMART AS IT IS STRONG

With proven trailering technology, advanced trailering tech and eight available cameras† offering up to 14 views, Silverado provides everything you need for a long day of towing. Plus, the available Trailering App offers customized trailer profiles, pre-departure checklists and trailer tire pressure/temperature monitoring.†

Trailer Side Blind Zone Alert†

Trailer Side Blind Zone Alert enables visual side-mirror alerts when a moving vehicle is detected in the side blind zone — almost like having trailer-length eyes in the back of your head.

Trailering App

Whether you’re in it for the long haul or the short, you can create a custom trailer profile to help you easily set up and monitor your connected trailer.

ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY

SUPER CRUISE™ HANDS-FREE DRIVING

Available on High Country, Super Cruise† connected by OnStar is a hands-free driver assistance technology that manages speed and steering, and helps you feel energized and refreshed during your adventures — even while towing. With over 140 million miles of hands-free driving in our rearview mirror, Super Cruise with towing is the most advanced hands-free driving system in its class.†

Super Cruise™ with Trailering is unparalleled

Super Cruise allows you to tow your heavy load hands-free. When pulling a trailer, Super Cruise will automatically adjust the gap between you and the vehicle ahead for extra stopping distance, giving you added comfort and confidence for those long hauls. No other competitor offers a hands-free driving system with towing capability.†
